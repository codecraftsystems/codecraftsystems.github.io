The Advantage

WHY THIS
MATTERS

Most tools just give you links. DevCareer AI tells you what each link is — and ranks them so the best opportunities always surface first.

🎯
Direct Apply = 3× Better Odds

Applying on a company's own careers page means your CV lands in a smaller, more focused pool. Far fewer applicants, better visibility, higher conversion rate.

🤖
Stack-Aware Keyword Gen

AI generates queries specific to your exact tech stack. A Laravel developer gets completely different, more targeted results than a React or Python engineer.

⚡
Live Results, Never Cached

SearXNG fetches live listings every run. No stale jobs from 3 weeks ago — every result was posted recently and is actively accepting applicants right now.

🔒
Zero Account Required

No sign-up, no OAuth, no tracking. Your profile stays in your browser. The AI pipeline runs server-side with no personal data stored or logged anywhere.

🌍
Every Dev Stack Supported

Laravel, React, Python, DevOps, Mobile, Data Science — the AI adapts its keyword strategy to your specific discipline and experience level automatically.

📊
Match Score Ranking

Every result gets a percentage match based on how closely the role aligns with your profile. Best matches always appear first — zero manual sorting needed.
