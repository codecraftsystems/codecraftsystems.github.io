About This Tech News Feed

The CodeCraft Systems Technology News Feed is a live, real-time aggregator that curates the latest headlines from across the global technology landscape. Powered by the newsdata.io API, this page auto-detects your location and surfaces the most relevant tech news from your country alongside worldwide coverage.

Whether you're a developer staying up to date with new frameworks, a founder tracking the startup ecosystem, or a tech enthusiast following the latest gadget launches — this feed delivers fresh, unfiltered technology journalism in one place. Articles update every time you visit, ensuring you always see the most current stories.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the CodeCraft Systems Tech News Feed?
The CodeCraft Systems Tech News Feed is a live, auto-updated technology news aggregator that fetches the latest articles in AI, cybersecurity, startups, gadgets, and software development from around the world using the newsdata.io API. It automatically detects your country and shows relevant local and global tech news.
How often is the tech news updated?
The news feed updates in real-time every time you visit the page, pulling the latest technology headlines from newsdata.io. Articles are sorted by publication date, with the newest stories shown first. Refreshing the page will always load the most current news available.
Which technology categories are covered?
The feed covers Artificial Intelligence (AI), machine learning, startups and venture capital funding, cybersecurity threats and patches, smartphone and gadget launches, software development, cloud computing (AWS, GCP, Azure), open source projects, and developer tools and frameworks.
Can I filter tech news by category?
Yes. You can filter articles by Top Stories, Technology, Business, or only articles with images using the filter bar at the top of the news grid. The article count updates automatically as you switch between filters.
Who maintains this tech news feed?
The tech news feed is maintained by CodeCraft Systems, a full-stack web development agency based in India, specializing in Laravel, Vue.js, Nuxt.js, and Supabase. The feed is part of the broader CodeCraft developer ecosystem, which also includes a job board, a developer directory, and AI-powered job search tools.
